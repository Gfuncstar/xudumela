No. 01 · Self‑pitch

Camp Site.

↳ 19° 16′ 24.0″ S · 22° 50′ 51.4″ E

Grass‑and‑sand stands under leadwood and jackalberry, set back from the channel's edge by sixty paces. Eight pitches in total, each with its own fire pit, table, and tap. Shared ablutions sit a minute's walk away under a thatch roof. Charging is at the main deck, three minutes through the bush.

No. 02 · Walk‑in

Safari Tent.

↳ 19° 16′ 20.6″ S · 22° 50′ 42.2″ E

Six raised canvas tents on teak decks, named for the species most commonly seen from the porch. Twenty‑six square metres of internal space, en‑suite shower with hot water from gas, double or twin configuration on request. Mosquito netting on every side. Each tent has its own paraffin lamp, packed‑lunch hook, and a hammock above the deck.
