↳ Getting here

Your route to the delta.

Xudumela sits on the Xudum Channel in the Okavango Delta, northwest Botswana. The nearest gateway is Maun International Airport — a 25-minute transfer from camp.

✈ Primary gateway
Maun International
MUB · Maun, Botswana
25 min transfer to camp

Daily scheduled flights from Johannesburg, Cape Town, and Gaborone. Fly-in guests are met at the terminal and transferred directly to camp.

✈ International hub
OR Tambo International
JNB · Johannesburg
~2 hr flight to MUB

Primary international arrival point for southern Africa. Multiple daily connections onward to Maun via Airlink and other regional carriers.

✈ Alternative
Kasane Airport
BBK · Chobe region
~3.5 hr overland

Gateway to Chobe National Park. Charter connections available. The overland transfer passes through the north-eastern delta fringe.

→ Overland
Gaborone to Maun
A3 via Francistown
9 hr drive · or fly

Self-drive is possible in a 4×4. The route crosses the Kalahari and enters the delta region north of Maun. Flying is recommended for most guests.
